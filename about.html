Scratch Map well, a virtual one at least

About

The Scratch Map is a visual method for recording all the countries you've visited in the world*.

The virtual Scratchmap is inspired by its real life counterparts. This version however, is somewhat easier to take traveling with you.

You can browse around the the map and select countries of interest. You will then be presented with some basic data about that country, and will be able to mark it as visited. The app also calculates a rough percentage of the would you have seen, and displays this as a pie chart.

It was designed and built in the Autum/Winter of 2013 by Liam Richardson (with some help), and this version was gifted to Rowena Farr for Christmas of that year.

May she use it to record her travels far and wide. Merry Christmas.x

* Sorry astronauts, it only applies to Earth.

Scratch Map well, a virtual one at least

Colophon

The ScratchMap is a visual method for recording all the countries you've visited in the world*.

The map is powered by jVectorMap, and SVG Mapping plugin.

The Country List was based on the GDP Tutorial, but updated somewhat by myself with the missing regions.

The country specific data is called from the Rest Countries API, based on ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 country codes. We use this to retrieve Name, Currency, Population and Timezone information.

The calculations for countries visited are based on the same data source that powers the Map. The percentage is rounded to the nearest whole figure. The pie-chart is powered by the chart.js libary.

* Sorry astronauts, it only applies to Earth.
